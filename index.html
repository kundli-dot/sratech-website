FAQ

Questions, answered

How long does a typical project take?
Websites usually ship in 4–8 weeks. Custom platforms and mobile apps range from 8–20 weeks depending on scope. You get a firm timeline before we start, not after.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes. We're a remote-first team and work across time zones with clients in India, the US, UK, UAE and Australia. We overlap at least four hours with your working day.
What is your pricing model?
Fixed-price for well-defined scopes (see our plans above) and monthly retainers for ongoing work. You'll always see the full cost before signing — we don't bill surprises.
Will I own the code and assets?
Completely. On final payment, all source code, design files, and repository access transfer to you. No vendor lock-in and no licence fees to us.
Do you provide ongoing support?
Every plan includes a support window after launch. Beyond that we offer monthly maintenance covering updates, monitoring, backups and small feature work.
